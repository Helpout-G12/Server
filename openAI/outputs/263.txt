 The 8th edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL) is set to kick off on March 29th, 2019. As usual, cricket fans are eagerly awaiting the start of IPL 2019 and here’s what they can expect from this year’s tournament.
The IPL 2019 Schedule:
This season’s IPL schedule will feature 60 matches spread over 51 days, with the playoffs taking place at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai on May 24th and 26th, 2019. All the matches will be played across 8 venues in India, while a total of 73 Indian players will compete in this year’s tournament.
The teams taking part in IPL 2019 include the defending champions, the Mumbai Indians, along with the Chennai Super Kings and the Delhi Capitals who were both reinstated after being banned for two years due to corruption charges. Other teams participating this season are: the Rajasthan Royals, the Royal Challengers Bangalore, the Sunrisers Hyderabad and the Kings XI Punjab.
